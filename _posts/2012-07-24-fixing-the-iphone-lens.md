A few months ago my iPhone lens ended up being scratched after falling on the ground. I don&#8217;t recall when, it definitely happened after a few adult beverages, and it essentially made it impossible to take a good picture with my phone.

[<img src="http://blog.pintozzi.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/20120724-222618.jpg" alt="20120724-222618.jpg" class="alignnone size-full" />][1]

On Amazon I ordered a replacement lens kit for about $5. It came with a few pentelop screw drivers, which are required if you want to take the screw out of the bottom of an iPhone.

[<img src="http://blog.pintozzi.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/20120724-221826.jpg" alt="20120724-221826.jpg" class="alignnone size-full" />][2]

After removing the bottom screws, the back of the iPhone case slides up and you can easily get to the internals.

[<img src="http://blog.pintozzi.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/20120724-221923.jpg" alt="20120724-221923.jpg" class="alignnone size-full" />][3]

It took les time to replace the lens itself than it did to take the back of the phone off.

[<img src="http://blog.pintozzi.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/20120724-222145.jpg" alt="20120724-222145.jpg" class="alignnone size-full" />][4]

After installing the new lens and putting the back on, I can take clear pictures again!

[<img src="http://blog.pintozzi.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/20120724-222402.jpg" alt="20120724-222402.jpg" class="alignnone size-full" />][5]

Definitely an easy fix, and I&#8217;m happy I did it myself instead of paying someone else $50 to repair it for me!
