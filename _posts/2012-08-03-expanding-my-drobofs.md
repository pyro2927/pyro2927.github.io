A while back I bought a [DroboFS][1].  It has treated me well, and over time I have expanded it&#8217;s capacity by adding larger hard drives.  Data Robotics even hosts a nice [capacity calculator][2] so you don&#8217;t need to guess how much overhead their &#8220;BeyondRAID&#8221; will require.<figure id="attachment_487" style="width: 584px;" class="wp-caption aligncenter">

I started up the rebuild right before I left for work, so after a full work day the RAID should be fully rebuilt.  I just have to hope a second drive doesn&#8217;t crash during the rebuild process, as I&#8217;d lose all of my data.  Fingers crossed!
